1. The Claw Grip

The most important safety technique is the "claw grip." Curl your fingertips inward on your non-dominant hand, holding the food. Your knuckles should guide the side of the knife blade. This keeps your fingertips safe and away from the cutting edge.

3. How to Mince Garlic

Crush the garlic clove with the flat side of your knife to easily remove the skin. Roughly chop the garlic, then sprinkle a tiny bit of salt over it. The salt acts as an abrasive. Rock your knife back and forth over the garlic and salt until it forms a fine paste.
