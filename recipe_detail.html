Spicy Garlic Chicken Stir-Fry

By: Jane Doe

Quick Facts

Prep Time:

15 Min

Cook Time:

20 Min

Servings:

4 People

Category:

Dinner
View Reviews (15)

A flavorful and quick stir-fry recipe, perfect for a busy weeknight. Customize the spice level to your liking!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and marinate with soy sauce and sesame oil for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add garlic, bell pepper, and broccoli to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan. Stir everything together and serve immediately over rice.
